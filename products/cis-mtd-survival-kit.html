Questions

Do I need this if I already use accounting software?

Maybe. If your software handles CIS reconciliation properly (FreeAgent, QuickBooks), you're sorted. But many don't — they let you enter gross income but don't track CIS deductions separately. This kit shows you what to check and how to fix it.

I earn less than £50k. Do I need MTD?

Not yet. MTD rolls out in stages: £50k+ in April 2026, £30k+ in April 2027, £20k+ in April 2028. But if you're close to the threshold, start preparing now — HMRC penalties kick in immediately.

Can I use this for Self-Assessment too?

Yes. The CIS tracker feeds directly into your Self-Assessment return. It's the same numbers, just formatted for MTD quarterly submissions instead of annual.

What format is this in?
