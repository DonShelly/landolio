📌 Your Rights Under UK Law

Under the Late Payment of Commercial Debts (Interest) Act 1998, you can charge:

  • 8% + Bank of England base rate annual interest on the overdue amount
  • Fixed compensation: £40 (debts up to £999.99), £70 (£1,000–£9,999.99), or £100 (£10,000+)
  • Reasonable recovery costs if they exceed the fixed amount
