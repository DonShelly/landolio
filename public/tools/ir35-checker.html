IR35 Status Checker

Answer 12 questions to assess whether your contract is likely inside or outside IR35

⚠️ Important: This tool provides guidance based on HMRC's CEST principles and case law, but it is NOT a substitute for professional tax advice. IR35 assessments are complex and fact-specific. For definitive status, consult an IR35 specialist.

1. Substitution: Can you send someone else to do the work instead of you?

2. Control: Who decides HOW you do the work?

3. Control: Who decides WHEN you work?

4. Control: Who decides WHERE you work?

5. Financial Risk: Can you make a loss on this contract?

6. Equipment: Who provides the main tools/equipment?

7. Part and Parcel: Do you have the same rights as employees?

(e.g., access to employee perks, annual leave, sick pay, company events)

8. Exclusivity: Can you work for other clients during this contract?

9. Business on Own Account: Do you actively market your services?

10. Length of Engagement: How long is the contract?

11. Supervision: Are you supervised or managed day-to-day?

12. Mutuality of Obligation: Is there ongoing work obligation?
