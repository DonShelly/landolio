# UK Tax Deadline Calendar 2026/27
## For Freelancers, Sole Traders & Landlords

**Tax Year:** 6 April 2026 – 5 April 2027

---

## Self Assessment Deadlines

| Date | Deadline | What's Due |
|------|----------|------------|
| **31 July 2026** | Second Payment on Account (2025/26) | 50% of last year's tax bill |
| **5 October 2026** | Register for Self Assessment (new filers) | If you started self-employment in 2025/26 and haven't registered yet |
| **31 October 2026** | Paper tax return (2025/26) | File your Self Assessment return by post |
| **30 December 2026** | Online filing to get payment calculated | File online by this date to have HMRC calculate what you owe |
| **31 January 2027** | Online tax return + Balancing Payment + First Payment on Account (2026/27) | File your 2025/26 return online, pay any tax owed, plus 50% of next year's estimated bill |
| **31 July 2027** | Second Payment on Account (2026/27) | 50% of this year's tax bill |

## MTD for Income Tax (Mandatory from 6 April 2026)

**If you earn over £50,000 from self-employment or property income**, you must:

### Quarterly Submission Deadlines

| Quarter | Period | Submission Deadline |
|---------|--------|---------------------|
| **Q1** | 6 Apr – 5 Jul 2026 | **5 Aug 2026** |
| **Q2** | 6 Jul – 5 Oct 2026 | **5 Nov 2026** |
| **Q3** | 6 Oct 2026 – 5 Jan 2027 | **5 Feb 2027** |
| **Q4** | 6 Jan – 5 Apr 2027 | **5 May 2027** |

### End of Period Statement (EOPS)

**Deadline:** 31 Jan 2028  
(Submitted after Q4, finalises all quarterly updates for 2026/27)

### Final Declaration

**Deadline:** 31 Jan 2028  
(Replaces traditional Self Assessment return)

**Penalties:** £200 per missed quarterly deadline. Don't miss these.

## Corporation Tax (If You Run a Limited Company)

**Filing + Payment Deadline:** 9 months after your company's year-end

Example: If your year-end is 31 March 2026, you must file + pay by **31 December 2026**.

**Accounts Filing:** 9 months after year-end (Companies House)

---

## PAYE (If You Have Employees)

**RTI Submission + Payment:** On or before payday (every time you pay staff)

**Monthly Deadline:** 19th of each month (if you pay monthly)

**Annual P60:** By **31 May 2027** (for 2026/27 tax year)

**Annual P11D (Benefits):** By **6 July 2027**

## Penalties for Late Filing/Payment

### Self Assessment

| Offence | Penalty |
|---------|---------|
| File return 1 day late | £100 fixed penalty |
| File return 3 months late | £10/day (max £900) |
| File return 6 months late | £300 or 5% of tax owed (whichever is higher) |
| File return 12 months late | Another £300 or 5% of tax owed |

### Late Payment

| Delay | Penalty |
|-------|---------|
| 30 days late | 5% of unpaid tax |
| 6 months late | Another 5% |
| 12 months late | Another 5% |

**PLUS:** Interest accrues daily on unpaid amounts (currently ~7.75%/year).

---

## How to Never Miss a Deadline

- **Set calendar reminders** 2 weeks before each deadline  
- **Use accounting software** with automatic deadline tracking  
- **Enable HMRC email alerts** for Self Assessment reminders  
- **File early** — don't wait until 31 January  
- **Set aside tax monthly** — don't scramble to find £5k in January
