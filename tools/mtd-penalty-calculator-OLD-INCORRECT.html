How MTD Penalties Work

Late Submission Penalties:

  • £200 for each missed quarterly deadline
  • No grace period — penalties start from day 1
  • Maximum £800/year if you miss all 4 quarters

Late Payment Penalties (if you owe tax):

  • 5% of unpaid tax after 30 days late
  • Another 5% after 6 months late
  • Another 5% after 12 months late
  • PLUS daily interest on the unpaid amount

Example: Miss all 4 quarterly deadlines + owe £5,000 in tax (paid 8 months late):

  • Submission penalties: £800
  • Payment penalty (30 days): £250
  • Payment penalty (6 months): £250
  • Interest (8 months @ 7.75%): ~£260
  • Total: £1,560 in penalties + interest
