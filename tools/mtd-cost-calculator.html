How We Calculate These Costs

Software Costs

DIY: £10-15/month for basic MTD software (FreeAgent, QuickBooks Self-Employed, Crunch). Some free options exist but lack features.

Hybrid: £20-30/month for mid-tier software with better reporting (QuickBooks, Xero, Sage).

Outsourced: £30-40/month for premium software with accountant access (FreeAgent, Xero with advisor access).

Time Costs

Quarterly submissions: 1-3 hours per quarter (4x/year) depending on transaction volume and software familiarity.

Record-keeping: 30 minutes to 2 hours per month (depends on receipts, invoices, reconciliation needs).

Annual totals: 10-40 hours/year depending on your approach and efficiency.

Accountant/Bookkeeper Costs

DIY: £0 (you do it all) or £200-300 for annual review.

Hybrid: £50-100/quarter for bookkeeper review + £300-500 annual tax return = £500-900/year.

Fully Outsourced: £100-150/month for full bookkeeping + quarterly MTD submissions = £1,200-1,800/year.

Tech Confidence Impact

High: You'll learn software quickly (less time cost).

Medium: Moderate learning curve (average time cost).

Low: Higher time cost OR you'll need more accountant help.
